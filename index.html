About MATHDLE

MATHDLE is a collection of 11 mathematical puzzle games, each inspired by the deduction mechanics of Wordle but applied to different mathematical structures.

Design Principles

  • No Dominant Strategy - Multiple valid approaches
  • Partial Information - Each query narrows but doesn't determine
  • Mental Tractability - Humans can compete without calculators
  • Skill Gradient - Easy to play, hard to master
